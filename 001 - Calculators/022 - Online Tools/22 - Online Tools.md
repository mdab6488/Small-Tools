Random Number Generator
This random number generator generates one or many random numbers. The range of the numbers, whether there can be duplicate random numbers, and the order of the result can be defined by the user.

Dice Roller
This virtual dice roller can mimic the rolling of up to 5000 dice with any number of sides.

Subnet Calculator
This calculator provides the details of an IPv4 network and calculates subnets of the network.

Time Clock Calculator
This free time clock calculator calculates your daily hours on a weekly timesheet and can also deduct time taken for breaks. Hourly rate and overtime can also be included.

BAC Calculator
This calculator estimates a person's BAC based on their alcohol intake. It also provides the time needed for their BAC to drop within various legal limits.
